# 🦝 Jimothy's Seattle Adventure

A free, 16-bit style browser game starring **Jimothy** — Seattle's viral round raccoon from Ballard.

**[▶ PLAY NOW](https://pedrour.github.io/jimothy-adventure)**

---

## About Jimothy

Jimothy is a real wild raccoon living in Seattle's Ballard neighborhood who went viral in July 2026 after a video of him scurrying across a street racked up over 10 million views. His adorably round, compact shape is caused by a rare condition called **short spine syndrome**, which fuses the vertebrae and gives him his distinctive ball-like appearance. He was named by Kiana Hall, who said *"he just looked like a Jimothy to me."*

He's been honored with a bobblehead, a Google Search Easter egg, a Roku City cameo, tributes in World of Warcraft and Stardew Valley, and now — a web game.

---

## How to Play

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| `Space` / `↑` / Tap | Jump over cars, dogs & hydrants |
| `↓` / Swipe down | Duck under seagulls |
| `P` | Pause |

**Collect** fish 🐟 (+10), coffee ☕ (+5) and trash cans 🗑 (+3) as Jimothy waddles through Ballard. Grab the ⭐ **Hot Jimothy Summer** power-up for temporary invincibility and double points!

Speed increases every 300 points. How far can Jimothy go?

---

## Stack

Pure HTML/CSS/Canvas — no frameworks, no dependencies, no server. Single file, plays in any browser. All sprites are drawn programmatically with the Canvas 2D API. Sound effects use the Web Audio API.

---

## Share

There's a built-in **Share Score** button on the game-over screen that uses the Web Share API (mobile) or clipboard fallback (desktop).
